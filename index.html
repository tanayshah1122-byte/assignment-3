Life of Spice Packaging

A Packing design for a Indian Spice Blend Brand. Meant to be placed on 2.5 x 3.5 in glass bottles, the 2-tone based colourwave creates contrast to help showcase on store shelves. Indian symbolism is a major component of design, with the use of mandelas and geometric shapes.
